Fireplace Safety Tips You Need to Know

By SafetyExpert | Posted on October 24, 2023

Keeping your home safe while enjoying the warmth of a fire is paramount. Here are some essential tips...

  • Install smoke and carbon monoxide detectors.
  • Have your chimney inspected annually.
  • Use a fireplace screen.
  • Never leave a fire unattended.
